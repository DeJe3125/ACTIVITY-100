LABORATORY SAFETY RULES AND
REGULATIONS

  1. DO'S

    1. When entering the laboratory, take note of the following:

      1. Wear college ID whenever in the school premises
      2. Follow the queue when entering the laboratory.
      3. Secure personal belongings like cellphone, wallet, flash drive, notebook and
        pen. These things can be bought by the student in the workstation
      4. Place bag and other stuffs in the designated area.
      5. Be courteous.

    2. When entering the Laboratory, observe the following:

      1. Observe Silence. Avoid unnecessary movements.
      2. Check if the computer is working. Report to the subject instructor for
        malfunction/errors.
      3. Keep all electrical cords, wires and appliances away from water.
      4. When there is a need to go out, ask permission from the subject instructor.
      5. Be courteous.

    3. When leaving the laboratory, observe the following:

      1. Arrange the Chairs.
      2. Unplug electrical equipment after use.
      3. Clean as you go.

  2. DON'TS

      1. Do not bring food or drinks inside the laboratory.
      2. Do not damage, remove, or disconnect any labels, parts, cables, or equipment. More importantly, do not open the system unit.
      3. Do not install or download any software or modify or delete any system files.
      4. Do not leave your personal belongings unattended.
